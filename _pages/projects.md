This page lists some projects that I do aside from the research works in `Publications`.

I am developing efficient and reliable `neural representation algorithms for dynamic scenes`. Particularly I am interested in exploring the potential topics for categorical neural representations for foreground deformable objects and efficient neural rendering algorithms of large scenes.

Some earlier projects are `defect detection and segmentation on assembly line` and `small object detection in real-world construction site`.
